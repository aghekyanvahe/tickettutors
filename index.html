Our Mission

Ticket Tutors solves the widespread issue of traditional tutoring prices while simultaneously providing college students with a source of income that can be earned within the comfort of their dorm room

What Do We Provide For Tutors?

Unlike most jobs for college students, Ticket Tutors provides a unique experience in which money can be earned anytime and at anyplace. This enables students to work within their busy schedules while avoiding transportation costs associated with in-person jobs.

What Do We Provide For Students?

Instead of paying outrageous prices for tradional tutoring, students receive proper help at a fraction of the price. Alongside the price advantage, students are receiving one-on-one help from college tutors who have recently learned their respective material. We utilize the proximity of age between college and highschool students in order to maximize comprehension.

How Do We Foster More Educated Communities?

We at Ticket Tutors have recognized the large gap in educational oppourtunities between privilaged and less-privilaged communities. In order to bridge this gap, Ticket Tutors provides a service for underprivilaged students to receive free tutoring.
